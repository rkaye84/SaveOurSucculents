Easy Houseplants For Beginners

Are you a houseplant novice or simply someone with little time to invest in plant care who still wants to acheieve the green aesthetic? Then you are in the right place.

There are many small plants on the market that are very simple to take care of but will still do an excellent job at bringing life into any room you please. Read through our selection below and find the one that's perfect for you..

The ZZ Plant

The ZZ plant is a very low maintenance plant. Due to its waxy over-coat this fun plant often gets mistaken for being plastic. When caring for the ZZ plant, only water it when the soil is fully dry; if over-watered, it can turn yellow. It does best in bright to moderate indirect light but will do fine in extreme low levels of light. Needing very little water or light, this is the perfect plant for a student.

Aloe Vera

Similar to the other plants on this list, Aloe Vera is very easy to care for. Being a succulent, it does best in dry conditions, but they do need bright indirect sunlight. Only water the plant when the soil is fully dry. When watering, make sure the soil is fully soaked, but ensure that the water can drain freely from the pot. With its uses in natural remedies, this is great plant to help with burns.

Snake Plant

When caring for the snake plant, it is very simple. Ensure they are placed in indirect sunlight and do not water them frequently. Before watering them, make sure they soil has completely dried out and that they are in a pot that can drain freely. Much like the other plants on this list, this is a very easy plant to care for, so it is perfect for a beginner.
